Chili

Description

This dish is a basic chili dish, great for eating! Any dinner will become a good dinner if you include this delicious chili

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Onion (diced)
  • 1 Whole Tomato(diced)
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Can Kidney Beans
  • 1 Can Pinto Beans
  • Tomato Paste
  • Beef Stock

Steps

  1. Heat up olive oil in a pot, add onions and cook until you can smell them
  2. Add ground beef, cook until mostly brown
  3. Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, beans, and stock
  4. Cook for 20 minutes
