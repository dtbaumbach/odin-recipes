Lasagna

Description

This dish is a basic lasagna dish, great for eating! Any dinner will become a good dinner if you include this delicious lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning
  • (Optional) Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 450 degrees Farenheit
  2. Spray a Baking Dish with any Non-Stick Coating
  3. Line the Bottom of the Pan with Noodles
  4. Cover the Top of the Noodles with Sauce
  5. Cover with Cheese
  6. Repeat Steps 3-5 Until Pan is Filled
  7. Bake for 20-23 Minutes
